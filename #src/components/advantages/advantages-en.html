Why should you go with us?

  • We respect Elbrus

    Climbing Elbrus is a serious event that needs to be properly prepared. Prepare for both the participants and the organizers, because only joint actions can provide maximum result. We do not put the ascent on the conveyor, because then the risk of deficiencies. We only have a few dates a year, but we prepare well for them. And you too get ready. Only together.

  • Experienced Alpenist Instrustors

    With a group of 5 people or more, two experienced guides work, whose main task is not only to help all participants climb to the top, but also to ensure safety on the descent.

  • Warranty guarantee

    We do not set many dates, but for all the dates that we have, groups are recruited. If you bought a ticket, then you will go to the ascent.

  • Maximum safety

    It is safety that is a priority in all our ascents. We register groups with the Ministry of Emergency Situations, provide communication facilities and GPS, and we work with the group from two experienced guides.

  • Focus on the result

    We really want all of our participants to climb to the top, but this is not always the case. Much depends on the weather and personal well-being. We are really trying to help everyone ascend, but not at the expense of safety.

  • We take the whole organization

    We will help you with advice and business with preparation, with the selection and purchase of equipment.

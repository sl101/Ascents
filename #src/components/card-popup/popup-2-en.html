Climbing on Elbrus from the South by classic

Elbrus from the south is the dream of many climbers and travelers. Not everyone can boast of climbing to the highest peak not only in Russia, but also in Europe. Our trip is divided into two parts: preparatory, which runs through the Elbrus region and allows the body to acclimatize to high altitudes, and, in fact, the very ascent to Elbrus.

This is an ascent route from the south to the western peak. If you are new to the big mountains, we recommend choosing exactly this route. During the program, you will learn the basics of mountaineering techniques needed to climb the classic path. The entire route is very rich and interesting, because the paths pass through the most picturesque and amazing places The Caucasus. Join us!
